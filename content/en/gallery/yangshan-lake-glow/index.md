Recorded by the shores of **Yangshan Lake**, this image captures a sunset glow with profound depth and layering.

From a physics standpoint, these magnificent colors are the result of **Rayleigh scattering**, occurring as the sun's altitude angle decreases and light travels through a thicker portion of the atmosphere. The mirror reflection on the lake's surface and the drifting clouds on the horizon form a perfect **optical symmetry**, showcasing the atmospheric optical charm of the natural environment surrounding our campus.
