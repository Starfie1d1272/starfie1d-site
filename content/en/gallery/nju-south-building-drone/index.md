This project is an experimental exploration of "trajectories." Through a precise orbital flight of a UAV, a man-made "halo" was formed around the **South Building** during a long exposure.

Simultaneously, the stars in the background traced subtle arcs as the Earth rotated. By juxtaposing **man-made flight paths** with **apparent celestial motion**, this composition aims to explore the futuristic aesthetic of campus architecture through a scientific lens, presenting a surreal symphony of light and time.
