A milestone moment in my photography journey: this work was officially selected as the **featured hero image on the Nanjing University homepage**.

Utilizing a 2-second long exposure, the image captures the brilliant lights of the Xianlin Campus at nightfall, creating a striking contrast between the warm artificial glow and the deep blue twilight. More than just an aerial photograph, it serves as a visual interpretation of the "seize the day" spirit that defines our academic life.
